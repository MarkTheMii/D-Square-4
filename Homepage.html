UBISOFT CONNECT

Developed by Massive Entertainment™, Ubisoft Connect is a multiplayer & communications service that provides access to all games related to Ubisoft. Some of their most recognized titles include Assassin's Creed, Far Cry, Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six, etc.
* must have a created Ubisoft Connect account to access gaming services.


BlIZZARD (BATTLE.NET)

An internet-based online game, social network service, digital distribution platform created by Blizzard Entertainment. Battle.net was the first ever gaming service that was incorporated into games. Famous titles housed by Battle.net include Overwatch, StarCraft, World of Warcraft, Call of Duty, Diablo, etc.
* must have a created Battle.net account to access gaming services.


STEAM

A digital gaming distribution service developed by Valve Corporation. Steam offers features such as digital rights management, game server matchmaking, social networking & game streaming service. A few famous titles created by Valve and accessible through Steam include Counter Strike, Half-Life, Left 4 Dead, Team Fortress, etc.
* must have a created Steam account to access gaming services.


EPIC GAMES

A video game & software developer as well as a publisher, Epic Games is famous for its development of Unreal Engine: a powerful real-time 3D animation tool for immersive gaming experiences. Most known for titles such as Fortnite, Gears of War, Infinity Blade, and Unreal, Epic Games' hold an enormous influence on games across the industry.
* must have a created Epic Games account to access gaming services.
